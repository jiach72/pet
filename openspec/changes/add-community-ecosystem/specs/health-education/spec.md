## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Health Education Content
The system SHALL provide educational content about pet health metrics including HRV, heart rate, and stress indicators.

#### Scenario: Browse educational articles
- **WHEN** user opens education page
- **THEN** display categorized articles and videos with thumbnails and reading time

#### Scenario: Read article
- **WHEN** user opens an article
- **THEN** display formatted content with images, charts, and related articles

#### Scenario: Watch video
- **WHEN** user plays a video
- **THEN** open system video player with the educational content

---

### Requirement: HRV Interpretation Guide
The system SHALL provide guidance on interpreting HRV reports for pet health assessment.

#### Scenario: Access HRV guide
- **WHEN** user taps "Learn about HRV" from vitals dashboard
- **THEN** navigate to HRV interpretation article with examples

---

### Requirement: Heart Disease Early Detection Education
The system SHALL educate users on recognizing early cardiac warning signs from heart rate curves.

#### Scenario: View heart rate education
- **WHEN** user accesses cardiac education content
- **THEN** display visual comparison of normal vs abnormal heart rate patterns

---

### Requirement: Stress Indicator Education
The system SHALL explain physiological stress indicators and their meanings for pet wellness.

#### Scenario: Access stress education
- **WHEN** user opens stress education content
- **THEN** display explanation of HRV-stress correlation and calming recommendations
